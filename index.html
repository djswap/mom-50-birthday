🎉 Happy 50th Birthday, Mom! 🎉

In every stage of life, Mom put her family first. Skipping the best parts of every meal so her children and husband could enjoy them. Always the last to eat, but the first to serve love.

She is our Master Chef — pouring love into every dish. Her recipes are unmatched, her flavors unforgettable. Every meal was a memory.

When her son got married, she didn't just smile — she learnt to dance. At 50, she moved with the joy of a 20-year-old, stealing hearts all over again.

She cared for us when we were sick, stayed up late with worry in her eyes, celebrated every win, and stood strong through every loss. When we were unwell, she would quietly pray, asking God to take our pain and give it to her instead.

She cared for her parents, her in-laws, and everyone who needed her — endlessly, selflessly, and without hesitation.

Thank You, Mom ❤️

For every sacrifice, every hug, every laugh, every moment of love — thank you.
